### Website integration notes (this repo):
1. Put product photos in `img/products/<category>/…` and reference them in the
   `.product-card` markup (`data-name`, `data-brand`, `data-price`, `data-desc` feed the
   quick-view modal automatically).
2. Update the mobile **category grid** and desktop **mega-menu** links to the four live
   categories + new sub-categories above.
3. Instagram images export at 1080px — fine for product cards (`w=900` equivalent);
   for hero slides prefer landscape crops ≥1600px.
4. Add French `alt` text per product for SEO in the Tunisian market.

### How to source the images:
As the account owner you have the originals; use those (best quality). Otherwise:
**Instagram → Settings → Accounts Center → Your information and permissions →
Download your information** exports every post image + caption per account — captions
then double as product descriptions. Direct scraping is blocked without login, so
automated extraction is not an option here.
